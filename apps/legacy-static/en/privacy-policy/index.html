Log files

BrightHouse cleanup is performed according to the standard procedure for using log files. These files record visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and are included in the analytics of hosting services. The information collected by the log files includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service Provider (ISP), date and time, landing/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. They are not associated with any personal information. The information is intended for trend analysis, site administration, tracking user traffic on the site and collecting demographic information.

CCPA Privacy Rights (do not sell my personal information)

Under the CCPA, consumers in California have the right, among other things:

Request the company that collects consumers' personal information to disclose the categories and specific personal information that the company has collected about consumers.

Ask the company to delete any personal consumer information it has collected.

Require that the company that sells the consumer's personal information does not sell the consumer's personal information.

If you send a request, we have one month to respond. If you want to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

We would like to make sure that you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Each user has the right to:

The right to correction. You have the right to request that we correct any information that you consider inaccurate. You also have the right to request the addition of information that you consider incomplete.

Right to delete — You have the right to request that we delete your personal data subject to certain conditions.

Right to restrict processing — you have the right to ask us to restrict the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing. You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data subject to certain conditions.

Right to Data portability — You have the right to request that we transfer the data we collect to another organization or directly to you under certain conditions.

If you send a request, we have one month to respond. If you want to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
