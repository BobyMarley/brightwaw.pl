Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to popular questions about our services

How long does apartment cleaning take?

Regular cleaning of a 2-room apartment takes 2-3 hours. General cleaning takes 4-6 hours. The exact time depends on the area and degree of contamination.

What products do you use?

We use only professional eco-friendly products that are safe for children and pets. All products are EU certified.

Do I need to be home during cleaning?

No, it's not necessary. Many of our clients leave keys and go about their business. We guarantee safety and confidentiality.

How quickly can you arrive?

If specialists are available, we can arrive within 2 hours. You can also schedule cleaning at a convenient time for you.

Which areas of Warsaw do you serve?

We work throughout Warsaw and suburbs: Śródmieście, Mokotów, Ursynów, Wilanów, Ochota, Wola, Żoliborz, Praga, Bemowo, Bielany and other districts.
